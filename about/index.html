Personal Brand

Built on the ground.
Wired for systems.

I didn't start in tech. I started selling women's clothing door-to-door in the streets of Cairo — learning how to read people, handle rejection, and close without pressure. Before I was 20, I'd worked in a computer repair shop, a coffee house, a photography studio, and a field marketing team running nationwide campaigns for Mobinil across every governorate in Egypt.

That decade taught me something no course could: businesses don't fail because of bad technology — they fail because the people running them don't understand what they actually need. When I moved into AI consulting in 2024, I brought that with me. I don't recommend tools. I build systems that fit the human reality of Arabic SMEs.

The ITI Year & Going Full AI

In 2026, I completed a 438-hour intensive digital marketing program at ITI (Information Technology Institute) — covering SEO, paid media, analytics, and AI applications in marketing. Combined with certifications from Anthropic, Google, ALX, HubSpot, and IBM, and participation in Egypt's National AI Ambassadors Program, I now work at the intersection of real business experience and practical AI execution.

My edge isn't the tools — anyone can access the same tools. My edge is 10 years of understanding how Arabic businesses actually operate, and the ability to design AI systems that fit that reality without breaking it.
