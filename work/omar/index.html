01 · The Problem

One Logo Is Not Enough

The traditional branding approach involves a designer drafting 2 or 3 concepts for review. This narrow focus limits the creative exploration, forcing the client to choose from a limited list.

The core question: Which visual direction truly tells Omar Nasr's professional story? Not just finding the prettiest design, but identifying the visual system that represents his identity.

02 · Strategy

Exploration Before Selection

The AI-assisted approach reverses the branding equation: Instead of drawing 1 sketch and iterating, we generate 24 diverse concepts first.

Each style was selected to represent a different dimension of his persona: hand-drawn for authenticity, tech-driven for precision, badge formatting for professional credibility, and rough sketches for creative thinking.
