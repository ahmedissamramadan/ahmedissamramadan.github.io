01 · The Problem

Traditional Street Food, Professional Frame

The objective was to create a modern visual identity for a traditional Egyptian street food brand ("Foul w Falafel"). However, the layout needed to avoid looking generic or overly corporate.

The delicate balance: Modern, clean, yet authentic and relatable to the local Egyptian consumer.

02 · Strategy

Production Volume As Strategy

Instead of designing just 5 static templates, I built a custom **AI asset pipeline** to generate **55 high-quality variations** maintaining design parameters.

This allows the restaurant to swap visual assets for different campaigns, seasons, and social media formats without relying on a full-time graphic designer for every tweak. Consistency is maintained through strict prompt parameters.

03 · Workflow & Tools

Structuring the Asset Engine

01Style Parameters: Established color rules, bold typography formats, and textured background guidelines.
02Prompt Templates: Built reusable, highly detailed prompts for product packaging, promotional posters, and social stories.
03Batch Processing: Ran automated image generation batches, maintaining naming conventions for file management.
04Quality Curation: Curated the best outputs from each batch to compile the final library.
